Story

Our Journey

Oven Story was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to bring the joy of freshly baked goods to our community. What started as a small family bakery has grown into a beloved local destination for quality artisan breads, pastries, and desserts.

Our founder, Ravisha Santos, learned the art of baking from her grandmother in a small village in Kalutara. She brought those time-honored recipes and techniques to create Oven Story, blending traditional methods with modern flavors.

Our Mission

To create exceptional baked goods using premium ingredients, traditional techniques, and a whole lot of love. We're committed to bringing warmth and comfort to every customer's table.
